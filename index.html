"Do not despise this simple , lukavichak , pricey wretch , it was the brainchild of the harsh environment he was the victim of rough educators.
Thai evil is not in himself, but in the influence of the environment. Bai Ganyo is powerful, thoughtful , perceptive - mostly receptive !
Put it under the influence of a good leader, and you will see what a feat he is able to do .
Bai Ganyo is manifested so far only animals energy, but it is Thai large pool of potential spiritual force that expects only a moral impulse to become a living force ... "
