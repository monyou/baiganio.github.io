Bai Ganio is a fictional character & protagonist in
Aleko Konstantinov's series of satirical feuilletons.

"Do not despise this simple, sly, pricey wretch, he's brainchild of the harsh environment, victim of rough educators.
The evil is not within himself, but within the influence of the environment. Bai Ganio is powerful, thoughtful, perceptive - mostly receptive!
Put him under the influence of a good leader, and you will see what a feats he is able to do.
Bai Ganio is manifested so far only with his primitive animal energy, but he is keeping within a large pool of spiritual force, that expects only a moral impulse to become a living force...".
- by the feuilleton "Bai Ganio in Russia" - A. Konstantinov
